About me

I am a Research Assistant Professor at the Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, The University of Hong Kong. I received my Ph.D. degree in Information Engineering from The Chinese University of Hong Kong in 2025, supervised by Prof. Angela Yingjun Zhang. Before that, I received my B.Eng. degree from the Yingcai Honors College, University of Electronic Science and Technology of China in 2021, advised by Prof. Xiaojun Yuan. My research focuses on signal processing and machine learning for wireless communications, with a particular emphasis on the convergence of model-based algorithms and data-driven learning toward white-box and explainable AI solutions for future wireless networks.
